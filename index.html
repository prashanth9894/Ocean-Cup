ABOUT US

why we the are best

Welcome to Ocean Cup serving the finest coffee since 2010 at Marina Beach, Chennai. Our café blends the rich aroma of fresh coffee with the soothing ocean breeze for a relaxing experience. We use handpicked beans to ensure top-quality coffee, from classic brews to tropical-inspired drinks. With over a decade of expertise, Ocean Cup has become a favorite spot for coffee lovers to enjoy the perfect blend of taste and tranquility by the beach.
